Samosa

Our samosas, with their crispy exterior and flavorful fillings, paired with our signature chutneys, offer an unforgettable culinary experience cherished by all.


Pav Bhaji

A flavorful, buttery vegetable curry served with soft bread rolls, popular street food across India, bursting with spices and aromas, satisfying cravings with every bite.


Pani Puri

Crispy hollow puris filled with tangy tamarind water, potato, chickpeas, and spices, a refreshing and addictive street snack, loved for its burst of flavors.


Misal Pav

A spicy, savory curry made with sprouted lentils, served with pav (bread rolls), garnished with onions, cilantro, and lemon, a hearty and flavorful Maharashtrian dish.


Vadapao

A quintessential Mumbai street food, consisting of a spicy potato fritter (vada) sandwiched between soft pav (bread), served with chutneys. Its simplicity belies its irresistible taste, making it a beloved snack for generations, embodying the spirit and flavors of bustling Indian streets.
